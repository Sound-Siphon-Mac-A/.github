## 🖌 Overview  
Sound Siphon Advanced is a flexible audio routing and capture application that creates virtual audio devices on your system, enabling you to redirect the audio output of any application to any recording tool, streaming software, or processing chain.

Virtual device creation is straightforward. Define a virtual output, assign applications to route their audio through it, and that audio stream is immediately available as an input in any other application that accepts an audio source.

Application-specific routing lets you capture the audio of a specific app — a browser, a music player, a video conferencing tool — without capturing system-wide audio, giving you precise control over what gets recorded or processed.

The pass-through system ensures that routed audio continues to play through your speakers or headphones while simultaneously being captured, so monitoring is not interrupted by the routing setup.

For podcasters, streamers, musicians, and developers who need fine-grained control over how audio flows between applications, Sound Siphon Advanced is the virtual routing layer that makes complex audio setups practical without external hardware.
